Methodology & Sources

How we calculate your environmental footprint.

Core Formula

Our calculation engine uses a standardized approach based on the GHG Protocol.

Annual kg CO2e = (Monthly Activity Data) × (Emission Factor) × 12

To convert to tonnes, the result is divided by 1,000.

State-wise Grid Electricity (Scope 2)

Transport & Fuel (Scope 1 & 3)

Emission factors for direct fuel consumption and public transit are sourced from the India GHG Program and the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC).

  • Petrol: 2.31 kg CO2e / Litre
  • LPG (Domestic): 2.98 kg CO2e / kg (approx. 42.3 kg per 14.2kg cylinder)
  • Train Travel: 0.041 kg CO2e / Passenger-km

The Carbon Credit Score (0 - 850)

Your Carbon Credit Score is uniquely derived from the World Bank data for India's per-capita emissions.

  • India Per Capita Average: 1.9 Tonnes CO2e / Year
  • Score Formula: Score = 850 - ((User_Tonnes / 1.9) * 425)
  • Meaning: Emitting exactly 1.9 tonnes yields a score of 425. Emitting 0 tonnes yields a perfect 850.
